What Is the TOEFL iBT Test?

The TOEFL iBT test measures your ability to use and understand English at the university level. And it evaluates how well you combine your listening, reading, speaking and writing skills to perform academic tasks.

  • TOEFL is a standardized test developed to measure readiness for joining US and other international universities. It is also a prerequisite for immigration to certain countries.
  • TOEFL is designed to assess the abilities of ESL students to understand and use English fluently.
  • Over the years, different format of TOEFL have been developed, including: the Paper-Based TOEFL (PBT), the Computer-Based TOEFL (CBT) and the Internet-Based TOEFL (IBT). IBT is the latest TOEFL format and is now the only internationally-recognized TOEFL exam to be used in Egypt, after a period in which TOEFL exams were not held in Egypt.
  • IBT fees are USD 160 (one hundred and sixty US dollars) to be paid directly on the website of the TOEFL trademark owner.
  • The IBT exams are held in many Egyptian centers authorized by the TOEFL trademark owner. These centers include Professor which is an internationally-recognized testing center, the AUC and many other institutions and agencies.

Professor is an Authorized test center for ICDL Exam

  • The International Computer Driving License (ICDL) Commission was established in 1995. Having established wide reputation and recognition in the EU, the ICDL changed its name in 1997 to be the European Computer Driving License (ECDL) Foundation.
  • The ICDL was developed to enable individuals and institutions to improve their use of information technology and computers.
  • The ICDL has established many partnerships with the Ministry of Communications, the Ministry of Education, the Ministry of Health and Population and the Supreme Council of Universities. This has meant that ICDL will be available for a wide range of individuals to help them qualify for jobs and/or to develop job skills.
  • Professor for Training Courses is an accredited testing center for ICDL and you can take the ICDL exam in Professor headquarters in 6th of October City.
  • Furthermore, Professor offers up to 50% discount on the ICDL preparatory courses which test takers pass the examinations. Professor also provides models of the ICDL tests, from previous administrations to help learners practice to pass the ICDL test.
