Mini-Diploma in Soft Skills (MDSS) at Professor

Mini-Diploma in Soft Skills (MDSS) is an advanced professional diploma provided by Professor for Training Courses which is ISO-9001 accredited language learning and training center collaborating with the Atlanta Institute .
Professor is one of the largest training centers, in Egypt, offering training diplomas and courses concerning the development of administrative skills.
The mini-diplomas offered by Professor are fast track training courses focusing on the development of Managerial skills required for individuals to win job opportunities in large international and local corporations. An employee may acquire these skills through his / her work for any company, but this would take a very long time to be achieved and may cause troubles to such employee. Hence, it is through such mini-diplomas that employees can ensure acquiring these skills easily and efficiently within short periods.
The objective of such diplomas and mini-diplomas is, therefore, to boost administrative efficiency of every individual in corporations and administrative and educational institutions so that job tasks and duties can be handled in an easy and efficient way by those well-trained individuals. Furthermore, such courses address governmental sector employees, university students and training officers in corporations and companies.
